# AI Assistant & MCP Server

SparkyFitness includes an optional **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** server. This allows you to connect advanced AI assistants (like Claude Desktop, Cursor, or custom AI clients) directly to your personal health data securely.

When you enable the MCP Server, your AI assistant gains the ability to read your health logs, track your progress, and provide hyper-personalized coaching based on your actual data.

## Key Features

The MCP server exposes a variety of "tools" to the AI, categorized by function:

### 🕵️ Health Detective (Pattern Detection)
The AI can scan up to 90 days of your historical data to find hidden correlations between your diet, sleep, and mood. 
*   **15-Nutrient Tracking**: It analyzes everything from basic macros (Protein, Carbs, Fat) to micronutrients (Vitamin A, C, Calcium, Iron, Sodium) and compares them against your sleep score and mood.
*   **Example Output**: *"I noticed that on days where your sodium intake exceeds 2300mg, your sleep score drops the following night."*

### 🤖 Auto-Coach (Custom Planning)
The AI acts as your personal fitness coach.
*   **Dynamic TDEE**: It calculates your actual Total Daily Energy Expenditure by analyzing your recent calorie intake vs. your weight fluctuations.
*   **Macro Planning**: Generates customized daily macro targets based on whether you want to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain.
*   **Shopping Lists**: It can read what foods you've previously logged and generate high-protein shopping lists tailored to your historical preferences.

### 📝 Diary Management
The AI can act as your personal secretary. You can simply tell your AI client:
*   *"I just ate a 200g chicken breast and 1 cup of rice."* -> The AI will automatically search the database, do the math, and log it to your SparkyFitness diary.
*   *"Log my morning workout."* -> The AI will record your exercise duration and sets.
*   *"How's my streak?"* -> The AI can fetch your current consecutive logging streak and provide motivational nudges.

## How It Works Securely

1.  **Dual-Pool Database**: The MCP server uses a highly secure Dual-Pool database architecture. Authentication is handled by an admin-level connection, but all data queries are executed through an isolated application connection.
2.  **Row Level Security (RLS)**: Because the MCP server uses the exact same PostgreSQL Row Level Security policies as the main web app, the AI is physically restricted by the database from seeing any data that does not belong to your user ID.
3.  **API Keys**: You must generate a personal `SPARKY_FITNESS_API_KEY` in your SparkyFitness dashboard and provide it to your AI client. The AI client passes this key to the MCP server with every request.

## How to Enable

If you are using Docker Compose, the MCP Server is commented out by default.
Simply open your `docker-compose.yml` file, uncomment the `sparkyfitness-mcp` service, and run `docker compose up -d`. 

Then, configure your AI client (like Claude Desktop) to connect to the server using the API key you generated!
